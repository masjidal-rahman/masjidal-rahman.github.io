About

Islamic Center for Muslims to Achieve Spiritual Goals

At Masjid Al-Rahman, we strive to create a space where individuals of diverse backgrounds feel comfortable and respected, including people from different ethnic, racial, and socioeconomic groups. This could involve implementing policies and practices that promote inclusivity and accessibility, such as providing translation services, accommodating physical disabilities, and creating opportunities for diverse voices to be heard and valued.

About
